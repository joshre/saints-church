# Jesus is our Peace

## Ephesians 2:13-16

**13** But now in Christ Jesus you who once were far off have been brought near by the blood of Christ. **14** For he himself is our peace, who has made us both one and has broken down in his flesh the dividing wall of hostility **15** by abolishing the law of commandments expressed in ordinances, that he might create in himself one new man in place of the two, so making peace, **16** and might reconcile us both to God in one body through the cross, thereby killing the hostility.

## Scriptures and Prayers for Peace

- **If they are overwhelmed by work or pressure**, pray that they would find calm and freedom in working for the Lord, not under the weight of human demands. *Colossians 3:23*
- **If they are facing conflict or strained relationships**, pray that Christ's peace would govern their words and actions, making them gentle and forgiving. *Romans 12:18*
- **If they are uncertain about decisions**, pray that God's Spirit would guide them with clarity and settle their heart with confirming peace. *Isaiah 30:21*
- **If they are fearful or restless**, pray that the Lord would grant them secure rest and confidence in His protection. *Psalm 4:8*
- **If they are struggling with health or weakness**, pray that God's sufficient grace would steady them and that His peace would replace discouragement. *2 Corinthians 12:9*
- **If they feel spiritually attacked or burdened**, pray that they would stand firm in the gospel of peace, finding strength to resist temptation. *Ephesians 6:15*
