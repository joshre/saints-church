Prayer

Family prayer

Prayer is our means of communicating with our Father. In scripture we see examples of praise, thanksgiving, confession, and petition.

We believe that our children can and should participate in meaningful prayer. Our family prayers are intentionally accessible to children while not dumbing down our theology or our reverence toward God.

Communal prayer

There is something powerful and beautiful about many hearts moving together toward God in prayer. Beyond our Sunday services, we gather monthly for focused prayer as a church community.

We pray for our church, our community, our nation, and the advancement of God's kingdom throughout the world.
